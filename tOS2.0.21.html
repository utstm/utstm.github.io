An award system to keep track of our contributions and growth

FAQ

Feel free to reach out any club officer for questions.

What is tOS?
It's a system designed to:
1 . Encourages you to reach your public speaking goals.
2. Celebrates members who are reaching towards their goals.
3. Develop strategies to reach your goals.
How it works
Simply put:
* Members will collect points for a variety of tasks, roles, or speeches they complete.
* Members will be rewarded for earning points, and winners can share their strategies with members and guests.

Our system is running on a monthly basis.
in the begining of each month, all members' exising toasty points will be reset to 0.
during each month, the system will fetch data from our signup sheet and keep updating everyone's toasty points.
in the end of each month, we will hold a celemony to celebrate winners and spread success experiences.
How to earn toasty points
There are several ways
* Sign up for meeting roles
* Give feedbacks
* Refer a friend

How to read the diagram?
One useful techinique to reaching goals is to visualize your success. Here, we visualize our progress using dots growing bigger in size.
In this page, we use one dot to represent each member. Active members (tosty point > 0) will be shown in yellow while inactive members in white. The more toasty points one has earned, the bigger her/his dot will be.
How to check details of one's toasty points
You can click the name and it will redict you to a timeline page showing where all the points come from.
What are the awards?
We plan to offer traveling trophies to:
  • Toasty of the month (exec, member, and guest)
  • Top 3 Mentor-Mentee pairs of the Month
Also, each year we offer trophies to:
  • Toasty of the year
  • Most improved Member
  • Best Speaker/Evaluator/TabletopicSpeaker of the year
  • Most improved Member
